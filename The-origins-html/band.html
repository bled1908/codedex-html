📜 About the Band:

Imagine Dragons is a Grammy-winning pop-rock band that blends powerful lyrics with energetic beats. Formed in 2008, they rose to fame with their debut album "Night Visions." Their music mixes rock, electronic, and pop elements, making them one of the most popular bands worldwide.
